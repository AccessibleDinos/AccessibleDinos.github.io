About Accessible Comics

Hello, I'm Brian! It is my goal, beginning with Ryan North's amazing web comic Dinosaur Comics to try creating accessible versions of web comics. The idea is to make it so that what is normally thought to be a purely visual medium (i.e., comics) is accessible to everyone, so that nobody has to miss out on these awesome things.

Please note, though, that I have created this for a school project, and I do not have any affiliation with Ryan North or his website. My focus here is on transcribing and adapting existing content into an accessible format, and I therefore cannot (and should not) take any credit for the actual creation of the comics.

For further information about creating accessible comics, please contact Brian.
