April 18, 2018 - Transcript!

There is a wide rectangle consisting of six comic panels stacked into two rows of three.


TOP LEFT PANEL:

There is a title in bold and all-caps centered at the top of the panel saying, "THE TOP FIVE EMOTIONS".

The green T-REX, viewed from the waist up, is talking.

T-REX: 1) Happiness!


TOP MIDDLE PANEL:

Focus on T-REX's face. His mouth is open wider.

T-REX: 2) Happiness, but with just a touch of melancholia!


TOP RIGHT PANEL:

The T-REX is stomping on a very small log cabin, which has a blue car outside of it. There is a yellow DROMICEIOMIMUS on the right edge of the panel, looking behind herself to see T-REX.

T-REX: 3) Megahappiness!

DROM.: Wait, why isn't that #1?

T-REX: It's a sometimes treat. If megahappiness was as common as happiness, then we'd have to rename it to "happiness" and the original "happiness" would, according to the standard metric of prefixes, become "micro-happiness".

DROM.: Fair enough!


BOTTOM LEFT PANEL:

T-REX is facing the left edge of the panel, stomping on a small woman. Behind him is an orange UTAHRAPTOR with his mouth open wide.

T-REX: 4) The feeling you get by putting your hands into a big bag of rice!

UTAH.: Ooh, that one's solid!


BOTTOM MIDDLE PANEL:

T-REX is on the left side of the panel, his head turned to face UTAHRAPTOR, who is on the right side of the panel, facing T-REX.

T-REX: That last one but, with a non-Newtonian fluid instead.

UTAH.: And that one's solid only under sufficient force! Well, buddy, your list is great and I have no criticisms for you. How about you and I both enjoy some emotion #1 right now?


BOTTOM RIGHT PANEL:

There is a title in bold and all caps in the top left of the panel, saying "BUT SOON:"

T-REX's whole body is seen, his arms are up, and his feet are pointed towards themselves. His mouth is open very wide.

T-REX: Oh no! I got a bit of emotion #2 in me too!!

T-REX: *LONG DRAMATIC SIGH*
